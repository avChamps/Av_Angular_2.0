What It Is

Projector throw distance is the value that helps in obtaining the best-quality picture results as formed by the projector on the screen. This distance can be calculated using a projector throw ratio distance calculator, which helps increase accuracy, save time and effort, and promise a high-quality and ultimate viewing experience.

Estimating Throw Distance Formula

The formula for estimating throw is: D = SW * TR

Where

  • D is the distance from the front of the lens to the closest point on the screen
  • SW is screen width or width of the projected image
  • TR is the throw ratio or ratio of throw distance to image width
  • Refer to the owner's manual of your projector and lens combination to find an accurate formula for your specific project.
